# Iran War Market Analysis
Date: 2026-03-15

## 1. Situation
Conflict between Israel, Iran, and US escalating.
Risk of Strait of Hormuz disruption.

Key concern:
Energy supply shock.

## 2. Market Reaction

Oil ↑  
Dollar ↑  
Stocks ↓  
Bonds → mixed

## 3. Key Macro Chain

War
→ Oil supply risk
→ Oil price increase
→ Commodity(원자재) inflation
→ Inflation expectations
→ Interest rate pressure

## 4. Asset Impact

Oil
Short-term bullish due to supply risk.

Dollar
Risk-off environment → Dollar strength.

Bonds
Two opposing forces:

1. Risk-off → bond buying
2. Inflation → yields rise

Therefore bonds show volatility.

## 5. Key Variable

Strait of Hormuz disruption.

If blocked:

Oil → $100+ possible  
Inflation → rise  
Interest rates → pressure upward

## 6. Investment Idea

Short-term:
Oil exposure

Mid-term:
Dollar strength possible

Bonds:
Uncertain due to inflation vs risk-off dynamics
